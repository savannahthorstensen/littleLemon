About Us

Welcome to Little Lemon, a family-owned Mediterranean restaurant nestled in the heart of Chicago, Illinois. At Little Lemon, we are dedicated to serving traditional Mediterranean dishes with a modern twist, blending the rich flavors of Italian, Greek, and Turkish cuisines.

Our chefs are passionate about crafting each dish with care and creativity, inspired primarily by Italian culinary traditions. This dedication brings a touch of the Amalfi Coast to every plate, offering a truly memorable dining experience.

Little Lemon is the proud creation of two Italian brothers, Mario and Adrian. Their journey began when they moved to the United States to fulfill a shared dream of owning a restaurant. Mario, with his extensive experience as a chef in Italy, brings authentic family recipes to our menu. Meanwhile, Adrian, the driving force behind our marketing, has expanded our offerings to include a variety of Mediterranean delights, ensuring there is something for everyone.

Join us at Little Lemon to savor the vibrant and diverse flavors of the Mediterranean, and experience a taste of Italy right here in Chicago.
